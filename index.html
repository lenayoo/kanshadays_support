Support & Privacy

Gratitude Diary is a simple journaling app. Your entries are stored only on your device. We do not collect, track, or share personal data.

Privacy Policy

Data collection: None.

Data storage: Your journal entries are saved locally on your device (on-device storage).

Data sharing: We do not transmit your entries to any server. We do not sell or share data with third parties.

What we do NOT do

  • No account creation
  • No analytics / tracking
  • No ads
  • No cloud sync (your data stays on your device)

If you delete the app or reset your device, locally stored data may be removed depending on your device settings.
